About me

I’ve worked in a European project for the EIT from the Polytechnic University of Madrid, building a training environment for student-surgeons, consisting of a 3D laparoscopy simulator and a set of web-based tools to visualize the simulator metrics.

During my internship I improved the company workflow reducing one-week tasks to around two-days tasks. As R&D engineer, I led the migration from a monolithic system to a RESTful based one, saving in 75% code to maintain and was responsible for maintaining legacy C++ based projects, fixing memory-leaks, update codebase to the last standard version.

I always looking for new challenges and opportunities, my field of knowledge are focused, but not limited, to computer graphics, take a look at my work. Right now I am seeking for a new job position but, as I keep trying to improve my skills, my knowledge of C++ and technology in general.
