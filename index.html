About

I'm a software engineer, who enjoys building interactive and visual experiences and tools, specialized in C++ and 3D technologies.

I also have experience building web-apps, where I developed skills around design, UI/UX, data manipulation, RESTful APIs and databases.

I’ve worked in a EIT-Health project, building a 3D simulator for laparoscopy tasks, tracking student-surgeons actions as metrics and displaying them as graphs in an evaluation web-app.

I led the migration from a monolithic system to a RESTful based one, saving in 75% code to maintain and improved companies workflows reducing one-week tasks to around two-days tasks.

I always looking for new challenges and opportunities, my field of knowledge are focused, but not limited, to computer graphics.
