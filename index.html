About Me

I'm Daniel and I'm from Galicia. I'm a committed, hard-working and fast-learner. I could define myself as a backend and graphics developer. I know where my limits are and I know how to work as a team so I never say no to new challenges!

I get my B.Tech CSE at the EQAINE sealed University of Vigo and then, I moved to Madrid to specialise in low level graphics at the URJC with a Master in Computer Graphics, Games and Virtual Reality.

My main languages are C++, Go and Python and and I have a wide knowledge in the technologies OpenGL, GLSL, Unity and QT5 (python version) among others. Although I also have knowledge in vanilla web technologies HTML5+CSS3+JS to create simple but effective tools and always betting on a minimalistic aesthetics.

From June 2018 to June 2019 I've been one of the main developers in an EIT-Healt project where we created a tool using Unity3D for the Design and Execution of M.I.S surgery tasks, named MIS-SIM. Where my role also implied the creation of the backend: RESTful API, User auth and Licensing system.

When I'm out on the keyboard I'm usually behind my camera, visiting new places, breathing fresh air and also researching how to continue improving my work.

Faithful to the philosophy of never settle.
