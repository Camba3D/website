About Me

I'm Daniel and I'm from Galicia. I am passionate about computers since I have memory use. I'm a committed, hard-working and fast-learner. I could define myself as a backend and graphics developer. But it would be more correct to define myself as a problem solver. I know where my limits are and I know how to work as a team so I never say no to new challenges!

I studied computer engineering at the University of Vigo which has been awarded the EQAINE seal and I moved to Madrid to specialise in low level graphics at the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos with a Master in computer graphics, games and virtual reality.

My main languages are C++, Go and Python and and I have a wide knowledge in the technologies OpenGL, GLSL, Unity and QT5 (python version) among others.
Although I also have knowledge in vanilla web technologies HTML5+CSS3+JS to create simple but effective tools and always betting on a clean aesthetics. In addition to a lot of experience in building servers, always working as a backend developer mainly.

From June 2018 to June 2019 I've been one of the main developers in an European project of the EIT-Healt where we created a tool for the design and execution of minimally invasive surgery tasks. Where my role was mainly the creation of the backend of the tool with a RESTful API architecture with user verification and licensing system for the installation of the application. In addition to the main web of the project, a tool for visualization of metrics and how support in the development of the tool in Unity3D.

When I'm not on the keyboard I'm usually behind my camera, visiting new places, breathing fresh air and also researching how to continue improving my work.

Faithful to the philosophy of never settle.
